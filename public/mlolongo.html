JUJO Heights

JUJO Heights, Mlolongo

A compact, well-run estate on the busy side of the Nairobi corridor — built for people who want predictable access, resident parking, and a security presence at the gate. This is not the Syokimau site; that estate is Blessed Haven under the same operator.

Living here

JUJO Heights Mlolongo is positioned for daily commuting: when traffic allows, you’re within practical reach of Nairobi CBD, industrial zones, and the wider expressway network. The tone of the block is urban and efficient — clear rules, visible security, and parking thought through for car-owning households.

We’ll add unit mix, sizes, and indicative rent once you supply them — see the checklist CONTENT_NEEDED.md in the project folder for what to send us.
