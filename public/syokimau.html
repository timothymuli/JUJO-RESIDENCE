Blessed Haven, Syokimau

Blessed Haven sits toward the back of TPA Court in Syokimau — quieter than the frontage roads, with more green around the block and a children’s play area for resident families. It is a separate estate from JUJO Heights Mlolongo; both are operated under JUJO Residence.

Living here

Syokimau suits households who want space and calm but still need JKIA, the Nairobi Expressway, or south-side meetings within reach. The “back” position inside TPA Court usually means less through-traffic past your front door — confirm exact access on your first visit.
