Nairobi corridor · two distinct estates

Two addresses. Two identities. One standard of care.

JUJO Heights sits in Mlolongo — geared for commuters who want parking, controlled access, and a short run into town. Blessed Haven is in Syokimau, TPA Court (back) — quieter, greener, with space for families and a play area. Both run under JUJO Residence with the same systems: enquiries through this site, maintenance tickets, and rent lines visible to tenants who have an account.

How we’re organised

JUJO Residence is the umbrella you see on contracts and on this website. Each plot keeps its own brand and tenant list — so you always know whether you’re asking about Mlolongo or Syokimau.

Mlolongo

JUJO Heights

Urban edge of the corridor. Strong on access control, resident parking, and predictable handover for people who work in Nairobi or along the expressway.

Syokimau · TPA Court

Blessed Haven

Set toward the back of TPA Court — more breathing room, family-friendly touches, and links to the airport / expressway when you need them.

What’s shared: enquiry handling, staff training, maintenance workflow, and (where applicable) the tenant portal. What’s not shared: your lease, your unit, and your building’s day-to-day — those stay with the estate you chose.

Questions we hear often

Are JUJO Heights and Blessed Haven the same place?

No. JUJO Heights is in Mlolongo. Blessed Haven is in Syokimau, TPA Court (back). Different names, different pages, different tenant pools — both managed under JUJO Residence.

How do I pay rent?

Use M-Pesa Paybill with the numbers on your property page and in the tenant portal. JUJO Heights and Blessed Haven use different paybills — always pick the one for your building. The site does not run STK push yet; you enter Paybill manually in the M-Pesa menu.

How do I report a repair?

Sign in with your tenant account, open a maintenance request, and track status there.

How do I register?

Register with your email, Safaricom number, and password. You’ll get an SMS code to verify. The office may need to activate your account before you can sign in.
